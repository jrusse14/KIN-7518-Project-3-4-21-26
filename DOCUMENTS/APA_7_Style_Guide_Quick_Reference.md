# APA 7 Style Guide: Quick Reference for KIN 7518

## 1. Document Formatting
*   **Font:** Times New Roman (12pt), Calibri (11pt), or Arial (11pt).
*   **Spacing:** Double-spaced throughout (including abstract and references).
*   **Margins:** 1-inch margins on all sides.
*   **Page Numbers:** Top right corner (header), starting on the title page.

## 2. Headings (Required for Reports)
**Level 1: Centered, Bold, Title Case**  
*Text starts a new paragraph.*
> **Introduction & Social Context**

**Level 2: Flush Left, Bold, Title Case**  
*Text starts a new paragraph.*
> **Data Collection Procedures**

**Level 3: Flush Left, Bold Italic, Title Case**  
*Text starts a new paragraph.*
> *Sample Demographics*

## 3. In-Text Citations
*   **Paraphrasing (Author, Year):** 
    *   (Smith, 2020) or Smith (2020) argues...
*   **Direct Quote (Author, Year, p. #):**
    *   "Social data is messy" (Jones, 2019, p. 45).
*   **Three or more authors:**
    *   (Taylor et al., 2018)

## 4. Reference List Examples
**Journal Article:**
Author, A. A., & Author, B. B. (Year). Title of article. *Title of Periodical, volume number*(issue number), pages. https://doi.org/xx.xxx/yyyy
> Clark, C., & Reese, A. (2023). Rivalry and representation in women's basketball. *Journal of Sport & Social Issues, 47*(2), 112-130.

**Book:**
Author, A. A. (Year). *Title of work: Capital letter also for subtitle*. Publisher.
> Sage, G. H., Eitzen, D. S., & Beal, B. (2018). *Sociology of North American sport* (11th ed.). Oxford University Press.

**Website / Social Media Data:**
Author, A. [Handle]. (Year, Month Day). *Title or content of the post up to the first 20 words* [Description of form]. Site Name. URL
> ESPN [@espn]. (2024, April 2). *Caitlin Clark breaks the record for most points in a tournament* [Instagram post]. Instagram. https://www.instagram.com/p/xxxxx
