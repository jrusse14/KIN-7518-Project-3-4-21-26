**RQ2 Unexpected Finding:**

- This is a fascinating finding worth exploring!

**Possible Explanations:**
- Volume vs. intensity trade-off: AR gets more appearance comments overall, but they're more varied (positive/neutral/negative mix)
- CC's fewer appearance comments may be concentrated among hostile commenters
- Different fan bases may engage differently

**For Your Final Report:**

- This doesn't invalidate your research—unexpected findings are valuable!
