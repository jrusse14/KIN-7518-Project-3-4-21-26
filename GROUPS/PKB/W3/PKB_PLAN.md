## 1. Research Questions & Significance

### RQ 1
**The Question:**  
How do Instagram comments differ in their framing of Angel Reese versus Caitlin Clark, particularly with respect to athletic performance, personal attributes, and legitimacy?

**The Context:**  
Women's basketball has grown in audience and publicity throughout the last years. The rivalry between Angel Reese and Caitlin Clark has been a central topic of conversation in women's basketball, which started while they were in college and continued over into their WNBA careers. The public discourse about them often extends into comments in social media platforms, such as Instagram. Thus, analyzing the comment section of their instagram posts can provide insights into the public discourse about them. 

**Why It Matters:**  
Analyzing the comment section of their instagram posts can provide insights into the public discourse about them. How athletes are framed in social media can have a massive impact on their career, marketability and public perception.
